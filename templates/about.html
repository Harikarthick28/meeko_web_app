The technology behind empathy

How Meeko reads between the lines to understand what you're really feeling.

Multi-Signal Detection

Traditional mood apps ask you to smile at a camera. That's not how emotions work — people mask feelings constantly. Meeko takes a different approach by combining multiple signals for a more honest reading.

Text Analysis

Powered by Google Gemini, we understand nuance — sarcasm, context, and the meaning behind what you write.

Facial Recognition

Computer vision detects micro-expressions, but we weight this lower because people often hide behind expressions.

Signal Fusion

When signals conflict, text gets priority. Your words carry more emotional truth than a snapshot of your face.

Contextual AI

"I love pizza" isn't romantic love. "I'm fine" often isn't fine. Our AI understands that difference.

Music That Matches

We don't just play "happy songs" when you're happy. Sometimes sadness needs gentle comfort; other times it needs energy. Our recommendation engine considers what your emotional state actually needs.

With support for 16 languages and 6 streaming platforms, you'll find music that feels right in the language you think in.

Privacy

Your emotional data is personal. Camera analysis happens in real-time and nothing is stored. Text analysis is processed through the API and not retained. We built this to help, not to collect.
